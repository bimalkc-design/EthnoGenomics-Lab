EthnoGenomics Lab

Integrating Genomics and Traditional Knowledge for Plant Conservation

Our lab focuses on organellar genome mining, species authentication, and biodiversity conservation through advanced bioinformatics and molecular biology, emphasizing Himalayan medicinal plants.

Explore Our Research

The EthnoGenomics Lab advances plant genomics by integrating ethnobotanical knowledge with molecular techniques. Our core focus includes organellar genome assembly, phylogenetic analysis, and conservation strategies for Himalayan flora.

Key research integrates bioinformatics for NGS data analysis, supporting sustainable medicinal plant utilization and biodiversity preservation.
