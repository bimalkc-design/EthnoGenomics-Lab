Research

I lead research in organellar genome mining of medicinal and herbal plants, combining field-collected vouchers, classical taxonomy, molecular lab work, and bioinformatics pipelines.

  • Organellar genome assembly & annotation (chloroplast, mitochondrial)
  • Comparative genomics for medicinal plant authentication
  • Development of bioinformatics pipelines and scripts
  • Ethnobotany and community knowledge integration
  • GHG inventory for vegetation & land use

DNA extraction, library prep (Illumina & Nanopore), hybrid assembly, annotation with GeSeq/PGA/MITOS, phylogenomics, Python/R scripts, NCBI/ENA deposition.
