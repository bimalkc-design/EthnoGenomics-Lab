Research

I lead research in organellar genome mining of medicinal and herbal plants, combining field-collected vouchers, molecular lab work, and bioinformatics analysis pipelines. Emphasis on reproducible workflows and traditional ecological knowledge integration.

  • Organellar genome assembly & annotation (chloroplast, mitochondrial)
  • Comparative genomics for medicinal plant authentication
  • Bioinformatics pipeline development
  • Ethnobotany and community knowledge integration
  • GHG inventory methodology
