About Me

As an academic and researcher, my journey is dedicated to **unlocking the genetic potential of plants**. My specialization lies in **organellar genome mining** of herbal and medicinal plants, merging the precision of **plant molecular biology** with advanced **bioinformatics** tools and deep insights from **ethnobotany**.

My work contributes to the conservation, authentication, and sustainable utilization of valuable plant genetic resources, particularly focusing on the rich biodiversity of Bhutan and the Eastern Himalayas.
